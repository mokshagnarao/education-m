Academic Offerings

Our Programs

Undergraduate & Postgraduate Schools

School of Computing

Computing, AI & Data Science

Master the realms of modern programming, practical AI integrations, deep machine learning models, database systems, and robust cybersecurity protocols. Choose pathways in B.Tech in CS, AI, Data Science, and BCA.

School of Liberal Arts

Arts & Sciences

Build an enduring foundation in cognitive science, social structures, and economics. We offer specialized B.Sc. (Hons) in Psychology & Neuroscience, and B.A. (Hons) in Literature, Economics, and PPE (Politics, Philosophy, & Economics).

School of Biotechnology

Technology & Sciences

Bridge the gap between biological systems and engineering solutions. Pursue B.Tech degrees in Biotechnology and Environmental Engineering, geared toward sustainability and real-world clinical or scientific problem solving.

School of Business

Business & Intelligence

Nurture your entrepreneurial drive and strategic capacity with BBA (Hons.). Specialize in highly sought-after domains like Business Intelligence, Marketing Research, Finance Analytics, Operations, or Human Resource management.

School of Law

Law & Governance

Gain a rigorous understanding of the modern legal system, policy-making processes, and societal regulations. Programs offered include B.A. LL.B. (Hons.) and LL.M. in Regulation & Governance.

School of Communication

Media & Visual Arts

Transform your creativity into structured professional skills. We offer a B.A. (Hons) in Media Studies and a B.Sc. (Hons) in Visual Communication, equipping you for roles in cultural curation, journalism, and multimedia design.

School of Medicine

Allied Health Sciences

Launch a high-impact healthcare career with highly specialized B.Sc. tracks, including Occupational Therapy, Cardiovascular Technology, and Clinical Embryology, combining rigid clinical training with modern medical science.
